Understanding and Treating Vine Plant Issues

Our AgriTech AI solutions help vineyard managers and home growers identify and treat common vine plant diseases, pests, and nutritional deficiencies proactively.

Disease Detection
Early & Accurate Diagnosis

Utilize AI-powered image analysis to detect common vine diseases like powdery mildew, downy mildew, and botrytis before they spread. Get immediate recommendations for treatment.

  • Powdery Mildew
  • Downy Mildew
  • Botrytis Bunch Rot
  • Esca (Black Measles)
Pest Control
Integrated Pest Management (IPM)

Identify pests such as grape berry moth, phylloxera, and spider mites. Our system provides tailored, eco-friendly strategies for control, minimizing chemical use.

  • Grape Berry Moth
  • Phylloxera
  • Spider Mites
  • Leafhoppers
Nutritional Health
Optimize Nutrient Uptake

Analyze soil and leaf samples to determine nutrient deficiencies (e.g., nitrogen, potassium, iron). Receive precise fertilization recommendations for healthier, more productive vines.

  • Nitrogen Deficiency
  • Potassium Deficiency
  • Iron Chlorosis
  • Magnesium Deficiency
Pruning & Training
Guidance for Optimal Growth

Learn best practices for pruning and vine training systems to improve air circulation, sunlight exposure, and overall vine vigor, reducing disease susceptibility.

Water Management
Smart Irrigation Schedules

Receive data-driven recommendations for irrigation based on weather forecasts, soil moisture levels, and vine development stages to prevent stress and promote health.

Environmental Factors
Climate Impact Assessment

Monitor local climate conditions (temperature, humidity, rainfall) and understand their impact on vine health and disease pressure, enabling proactive measures.
